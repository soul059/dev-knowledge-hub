## Course Overview

**Elementary Algebra** is the foundational branch of mathematics that introduces the use of symbols (variables) to represent numbers and express mathematical relationships. It serves as the bridge between arithmetic and higher mathematics, providing essential tools for problem-solving across science, engineering, economics, and everyday life.
