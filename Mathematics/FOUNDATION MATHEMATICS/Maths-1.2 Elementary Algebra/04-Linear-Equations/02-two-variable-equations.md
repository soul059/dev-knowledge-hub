### Point-Slope Form

$$y - y_1 = m(x - x_1)$$

Where $(x_1, y_1)$ is a point on the line and $m$ is the slope.

### Converting Between Forms

```
Standard to Slope-Intercept:
2x + 3y = 12
3y = -2x + 12
y = -2/3 x + 4

Slope-Intercept to Standard:
y = 3x - 5
-3x + y = -5
3x - y = 5
```

### Finding Intercepts

```
Equation: 3x + 4y = 12

x-intercept (set y = 0):
3x + 4(0) = 12
3x = 12
x = 4
x-intercept: (4, 0)

y-intercept (set x = 0):
3(0) + 4y = 12
4y = 12
y = 3
y-intercept: (0, 3)
```
