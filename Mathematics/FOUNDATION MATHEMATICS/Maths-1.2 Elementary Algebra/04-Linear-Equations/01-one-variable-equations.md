## 7. Equations with Fractions

### Method 1: Clear the Fractions

Multiply every term by the LCD (Least Common Denominator).

```
Solve: x/2 + x/3 = 5

LCD of 2 and 3 is 6

Multiply every term by 6:
6(x/2) + 6(x/3) = 6(5)
3x + 2x = 30
5x = 30
x = 6

Check: 6/2 + 6/3 = 3 + 2 = 5 âœ“
```

### Method 2: Cross-Multiplication

For equations of the form a/b = c/d:

$$\frac{a}{b} = \frac{c}{d} \implies ad = bc$$

```
Solve: (x + 1)/3 = (x - 1)/2

Cross multiply:
2(x + 1) = 3(x - 1)
2x + 2 = 3x - 3
2 + 3 = 3x - 2x
5 = x

Check: (5+1)/3 = 6/3 = 2
       (5-1)/2 = 4/2 = 2 âœ“
```
