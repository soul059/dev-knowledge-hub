### Identifying the Equation from a Graph

```
Given a graph, to find the equation:

Step 1: Find the y-intercept (where line crosses y-axis)
        Read the y-value at x = 0
        This gives you 'b' in y = mx + b

Step 2: Find the slope
        Pick two clear lattice points
        Calculate m = rise/run

Step 3: Write the equation
        y = mx + b
```

### Example 4: Medium - Find Equation from Graph

**Problem:** A line passes through (0, -2) and (4, 4). Find its equation.

**Solution:**
```

Step 2: Identify y-intercept

Step 3: Write equation
```
