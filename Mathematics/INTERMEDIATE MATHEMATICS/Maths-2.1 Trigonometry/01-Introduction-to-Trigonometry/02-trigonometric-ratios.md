### Reciprocal Ratios

The three reciprocal ratios are **cosecant**, **secant**, and **cotangent**:

$$\csc \theta = \frac{\text{Hypotenuse}}{\text{Opposite}} = \frac{H}{O} = \frac{1}{\sin \theta}$$

$$\cot \theta = \frac{\text{Adjacent}}{\text{Opposite}} = \frac{A}{O} = \frac{1}{\tan \theta}$$
