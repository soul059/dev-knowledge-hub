## Quick Revision Questions

1. **When should you send a thank-you email?**
   - Within 24 hours of the interview â€” it shows professionalism and reinforces your interest in the role

2. **What should you include in a thank-you email?**
   - Thank them for their time, reference something specific from the conversation, reaffirm your interest, and keep it to 5-8 sentences

3. **How many times should you follow up if there's no response?**
   - Maximum 2 times â€” once at Day 7 and once at Day 14; after that, move on mentally while keeping the door open

4. **How should you handle a rejection?**
   - Thank them, politely ask for feedback, express interest in future openings, and don't burn bridges â€” you may reapply in 6-12 months

5. **Should you send your corrected solution in the follow-up email?**
   - No â€” don't include your solution to the interview problem in any follow-up email; it comes across as insecure

6. **How do you build long-term professional relationships after interviews?**
   - Connect on LinkedIn with a personalized note, stay in touch occasionally, and engage genuinely rather than transactionally
