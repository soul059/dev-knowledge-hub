## â“ Quick Revision Questions

1. **What are the three phases of the 12-week plan?**
   > Foundation (patterns 1-4), Intermediate (patterns 5-8), Advanced (DP, Greedy, Mixed Review).

2. **How long should you spend on a problem before looking at hints?**
   > 20-30 minutes of genuine struggle before reading the approach (not the code).

3. **What is the spaced repetition schedule for DSA problems?**
   > Re-solve on Day 3, review on Day 7, re-solve on Day 14, final review on Day 30.

4. **When should you start mock interview practice?**
   > Starting from Week 5, once you have 4-5 patterns under your belt.

5. **What are the signs you're ready to move to the next pattern?**
   > Can write template from memory, identify pattern in < 2 min, solved 3+ medium problems solo.

6. **Why is talking aloud during mock interviews important?**
   > Interviewers evaluate your thinking process, not just the final code. Communication shows problem-solving ability.
