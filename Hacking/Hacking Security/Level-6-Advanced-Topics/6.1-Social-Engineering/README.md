# 6.1 Social Engineering — Complete Study Notes

## Course Overview

Social engineering is the art of manipulating people into performing actions or divulging confidential information. It exploits human psychology rather than technical vulnerabilities, making it one of the most effective and dangerous attack vectors in cybersecurity. This subject covers psychological principles, attack techniques, assessment methodology, and defense strategies.
