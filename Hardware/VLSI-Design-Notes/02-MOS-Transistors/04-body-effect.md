# Chapter 2.4: Body Effect

## ğŸ“‹ Chapter Overview

The **body effect** (also called backgate effect or substrate bias effect) describes how the threshold voltage changes when a voltage is applied between the source and body terminals. This effect is crucial for understanding stacked transistors and circuits with non-zero source potentials.

---

## ğŸ¯ Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:
- Explain the physical mechanism of the body effect
- Apply the body effect equation to calculate modified threshold voltage
- Identify circuit situations where body effect is significant
- Design circuits accounting for body effect
