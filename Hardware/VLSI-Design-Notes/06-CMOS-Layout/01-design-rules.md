# Chapter 6.1: Design Rules

## ğŸ“‹ Chapter Overview

**Design rules** are the manufacturing constraints that define minimum dimensions, spacings, and overlaps for layout geometries. They ensure the fabricated circuit will work correctly despite process variations. This chapter covers lambda-based rules, rule categories, and their physical origins.

## ğŸ¯ Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:
- Understand the purpose of design rules
- Apply lambda-based (scalable) design rules
- Distinguish between width, spacing, and overlap rules
- Explain the physical reasons behind design rules
