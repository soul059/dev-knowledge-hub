# Chapter 4.3: Complex Gate Synthesis

## ğŸ“‹ Chapter Overview

**Complex gates** implement multi-level logic functions in a single stage, reducing transistor count, delay, and power compared to using multiple simple gates. This chapter covers AND-OR-INVERT (AOI) and OR-AND-INVERT (OAI) structures, compound function implementation, and optimization strategies.

---

## ğŸ¯ Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you will be able to:
- Design AOI and OAI complex gates
- Optimize logic expressions for CMOS implementation
- Choose between complex gates and decomposed implementations
- Calculate transistor count and estimate delay for complex gates
